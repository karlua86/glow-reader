# Glow Reader — community post (2026-07-15)

Anybody here in need of a reader that works well with our glasses? With the help of Claude Code, I built this. Hope it helps. (I'm not from IT and can't code at all — Claude did all the programming, I just tested on my glasses and gave feedback.)

**📖 Glow Reader** — no glowing background block, just floating words. Reads **EPUB, PDF, TXT, MD**. Made for comfortable night reading: Low Light mode + 5 text brightness levels, adjustable text area, and it remembers your position and settings per book.

**5 reading modes** (press M to switch):
- 📄 **Page** — classic page flipping
- ⚡ **Word by word (RSVP)** — speed reading, one word flashed at a time, adjustable wpm
- 🎬 **Auto-scroll** — teleprompter-style flowing text, adjustable speed
- 💬 **Sentence by sentence** — one sentence at a time, manual or auto-play
- 📃 **Paragraph by paragraph** — same, but full paragraphs

**How to control it:**
- **No phone app needed:** any Bluetooth keyboard/mouse works (I use a free "Bluetooth Keyboard & Mouse" phone app). Arrows or W/A/S/D, SPACE to turn/play, press H for the full key list.
- **Glow Remote (companion phone app):** big buttons for everything + send books to the glasses in one tap. Needs Rokid Manager installed, and the glasses' WiFi on and connected to the **same WiFi network as your phone** — then tap "Find glasses" and it connects itself.

**Getting books in:** download them with the glasses' browser (they appear automatically), or push from the phone app.
